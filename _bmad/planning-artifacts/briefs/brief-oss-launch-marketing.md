## 1. Positioning — the one-paragraph version

For **product designers, design leads, and PMs working with AI across multiple tools**, **Duble Slash (//) is the AI cloud for AI projects.** Keep working where you work best — Claude Desktop, Cursor, Terminal, ChatGPT Desktop, Perplexity — and our agents quietly consolidate your **process, progress, and results** into one streamlined flow based on **FISH**, the double-diamond methodology for human–AI work.

Your process syncs across your machines. Your teammates pick up where you left off. Your raw sessions stay with you; your shared project state gets a visible face through **a Linear-style task view and a GitHub-style push-history view — UIs we built to make FISH projects readable at a glance.** Not integrations with someone else's product. Not new tools to learn. Just a familiar-shaped window into the work.

**Keep doing what you do best, where you do it best. We'll consolidate the rest.**

## 2. Three messages to lead with

1. **"Keep doing what you do best, where you do it best."** No tool switching. `//` works inside Claude Desktop, Cursor, ChatGPT Desktop, Terminal, Perplexity.
2. **"One streamlined process for all your AI work — based on the double diamond."** FISH turns drifting AI assistants into phase-shaped collaborators. Four phases: Explore → Solidify → Build → Ship. One shared vocabulary between designer ↔ PM ↔ dev ↔ future-you.
3. **"The AI cloud for your AI projects."** We hold your process, progress, and outputs. Sync across machines. Share with teammates. In V1, familiar Linear-style and GitHub-style views give your FISH project a visible shape — UIs we built to make AI project state readable at a glance. Not integrations, not new tools.

## 3. Three messages to kill

- ❌ "Nothing leaves your machine" — inaccurate. Process state, handoffs, and shared outputs DO sync to the cloud. What stays local is the raw session content.
- ❌ "Every AI session captured" — wrong scope. We capture **outputs + organized project context + process state**, not every message.
- ❌ "Productivity tool" / "AI second brain" / "privacy-first notebook" — wrong category. We're a method + agent cloud, not a notebook.
