## Copy-dev notes

- Hook structure is **parallel-stack** ("Claude for research / Cursor for code / ChatGPT for writing") — three beats of the same cadence builds rhythm before the payoff line.
- "Your AI tools are everywhere / Your AI work is nowhere" is a deliberate antonym pair — the ear remembers antonym pairs.
- "Type two slashes" is the operative verb — the one line where the product metaphor becomes tangible.
- "Explore. Solidify. Build. Ship." read as single words with hard stops. Each chip on screen lights on the corresponding word. Four beats = four phases = locked to FISH.
- "Your process syncs... your teammates pick it up on theirs" is two clauses, two machines — it's the only line that foreshadows V1 multiplayer without promising it.
- The manifesto close is the longest pause in the whole script (0.8s beat preceding). Let it breathe. The viewer should feel the film *resolve*, not end.
